Lasagne Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich sauces, and melted cheese. This version combines a creamy béchamel sauce with your favorite filling, such as meat, vegetables, or both, creating a flavorful and comforting meal. The pasta sheets are layered with sauce and topped with plenty of cheese, then baked until golden and bubbling. After resting, the lasagna holds together perfectly for serving, making it a hearty and delicious option for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pre-Cooked Lasagne Noodles
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Meet for Bolognesa Sauce

Step by Step

  1. First, melt the butter and add flour one large spoonful at a time. Stir until the butter and flour come together and form a kind of "dough". Then add plenty of milk depending on how much sauce you want. Finally, stir until the sauce forms; it should not be too liquid.
  2. Make your preferred sauce that will accompany the béchamel sauce.
  3. If you don't have pre-cooked lasagna, boil it in water for 10-15 minutes.
  4. TWith all the ingredients ready, add your preferred sauce to the bottom of a baking dish, then a layer of lasagna. The following layers can be arranged as you like (we recommend finishing with a layer of cheese).
  5. CREATE YOUR OWN LASAGNA!
  6. Once the lasagna is assembled, place it in the oven for 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is golden.
  7. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes so the lasagna doesn't fall apart when serving.
  8. SERVE AND ENJOY!
